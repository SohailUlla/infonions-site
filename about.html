About Infonions

We help people make confident and budget-friendly decisions by offering personalised guidance, trusted vendor connections, and easy-to-understand rankings and ratings across different locations. Our goal is to give every Indian clear options, honest comparisons, and practical support, so they can choose what works best for their needs without confusion or guesswork.

Our Mission

Our mission is to understand real living conditions across India and provide personalised, budget-friendly solutions that genuinely help people. We aim to publish transparent and reliable rankings and ratings that guide individuals, businesses, and governments to make informed, confident decisions.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower every person in India with a personalised life roadmap built on accurate, ground-verified insights. We aim to create a future where individuals can make confident decisions, improve their quality of life, and access opportunities with clarity and trust.

  • Personal consulting for everyday needs
  • Transparent district-wise rankings on living standards
  • Vendor matching that respects budgets and local context
  • Ethical, privacy-first data practices

Core Values

Accuracy

We ensure accuracy by validating every piece of information through trusted volunteers and multiple verification layers, giving you insights that truly reflect ground reality.

Privacy

We follow strict user-first policies. Your personal information is always kept confidential, and only anonymized insights are shared to ensure your privacy and security at every step.

Affordability

We focus on offering recommendations that are practical, accessible, and aligned with your budget—helping you choose the best solutions without overspending.

What We Do

Ground Data Collection

Our volunteer network gathers real household and vendor-level information including income patterns, local prices, services, infrastructure, and employment to reflect the actual conditions people experience on the ground.

Analysis & Scoring

We standardize data, assign scores, and visualize key indicators—covering living standards, infrastructure, job access, and affordability at the district level.

Personalised Consulting

We connect users with the right vendors and solutions that match their needs and budget, supported through chat, calls, and trusted local partners.

Volunteer Network & Field Verification

Our volunteer network is the backbone of Infonions. Volunteers collect verified, local data using simple mobile forms and small incentives. Data is cross-checked by supervisors before it becomes part of our analytics.

Volunteer benefits

  • Small stipends for verified survey work
  • Certificates and training for community data collectors
  • Priority access to vendor partnerships in their area
